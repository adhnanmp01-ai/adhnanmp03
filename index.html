About Infinomex

Infinomex is a professionally managed general trading and export–import (EXIM) company headquartered in India, delivering comprehensive international trade solutions across global markets. The company operates with a strong foundation in regulatory compliance, quality assurance, and ethical business practices. We engage in the sourcing, export, and import of a diversified portfolio of products, serving clients across multiple industries. By leveraging a reliable network of manufacturers, suppliers, and logistics partners, Infinomex ensures efficient supply chain management and timely fulfillment of international trade commitments. Our approach is driven by market intelligence, operational excellence, and a client-centric mindset. Infinomex is committed to building long-term strategic partnerships by providing consistent value, transparency, and dependable trade execution in every transaction.
